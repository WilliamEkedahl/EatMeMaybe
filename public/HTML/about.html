About the product

This project was developed as part of our group work at The University of South-Eastern Norway.
The goal of the product is to simplify and enhance the user experience with having more control over what foods they have bought and when said foods expire.
We have focused on user-friendliness, accessibility, and modern design principles.

In the video to the right you can watch a quick walkthrough of how you can use our product.
