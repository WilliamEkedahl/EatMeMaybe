About the product

EatMeMaybe is designed to help you keep track of what you have in your pantry, reducing both waste and expenses. Registering your groceries in the app will make it easy to monitor what you have, the quantity and the expiration dates. Whether you’re managing your weekly shopping or keeping an eye on long-term storage, EatMeMaybe helps ensure what you buy gets used in time.


Our algorithm estimates expiration dates based on food category, while the color-coded tracking system helps you stay on top of what’s still fresh. This way nothing goes to waste. Our goal is to make your life easier, while also promoting sustainability through better awareness of expiration dates. The app includes helpful features like filtering your personal inventory, viewing product details, adjusting quantities, and more. EatMeMaybe is perfect for students, families, or anyone looking to save money and reduce waste in a smart and easy way.


Still curious? Check out our video and see for yourself.
When you add products, their expiration dates are automatically set based on their category:
• Fruits and vegetables: 5 days
• Cooling products: 7 days
• Frozen products: 180 days
• Dry products: 240 days
Products display expiration date warnings using colours:
• Green: more than 8 days remaining
• Yellow: 7 days or less
• Red: 3 days or less
• Expired: o days left(expired)
